Mobile Expert

MonkeyPatch IT expertise company is looking for Mobile consultants. Are you a technophile and like technical challenges? Would you like to join a dynamic team passionate about IT?

Position and missions

As a Mobile Expert, you create and deploy native or cross-platform mobile applications. You will have as missions:

  • Contribute to mobile development in Android, iOS or cross platform
  • Propose new features and improvements
  • Provide technical advice and expertise
  • To be a force of proposal on performance and architecture
  • Ensure a permanent monitoring around the uses, technologies in the field of mobile
  • And (depending on missions and experiences) ensure the technical lead of the project

Within our team, you will also be required to regularly participate in technical events to confront new technical orientations and share your know-how.

Profile Wanted

You have a perfect command of one of the latest mobile technologies and you pay attention to making your developments the most efficient in a mobile environment.

You practice these technologies:

  • At least one of the technologies: Android, iOS, Cross Platform (Cordova, Ionic, …)
  • At least one of the following languages: Kotlin, Swift, Java, Objective-C
  • REST Web services
  • The ecosystem of your favorite platform (Gradle, Swift,…)
  • Unit tests, Integration tests….

Terms and conditions

  • Place of work: Toulouse
  • Compensation: Attractive
  • Type of contract: permanent contract
