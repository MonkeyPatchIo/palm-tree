DevOps Expert

MonkeyPatch IT expertise company is looking for Back-End consultants. Are you a technophile and like technical challenges? Would you like to join a dynamic team passionate about IT?

Position and missions

As a DevOps Expert, you are called upon to carry out development and operational missions. Your dual expertise allows you to create applications that are perfectly adapted to the infrastructure.

  • Set up tools to automate recurring tasks
  • Manage the continuous deployment platform
  • Propose new ways to improve the performance of development teams
  • Provide technical advice and expertise
  • Ensure a permanent monitoring around the uses, technologies in the field of Devops

Within our team, you will also be required to regularly participate in technical events to confront new technical orientations and share your know-how.

Profile Wanted

You are comfortable helping development teams to perform better, and if necessary, you are able to help them develop. You are familiar with these technologies:

  • Linux, Windows configuration
  • Security concepts for systems and applications
  • Automation languages (Bash, Puppet, Ansible,…)
  • Container technologies (Docker, Kubernetes…)
  • Continuous integration, continuous deployment
  • Cloud technology and clustering (Amazon, Google App Engine, Apache Mesos,…)
  • At least one programming language according to: Java/Groovy, Go, Ruby, Python

Terms and conditions

  • Place of work: Toulouse
  • Compensation: Attractive
  • Type of contract: permanent contract
